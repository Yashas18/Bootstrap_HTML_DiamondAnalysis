Diamond Price Distribution by Cuts

The Bin Size of the histogram is $2,000. Majority of the diamonds are priced less than $4,000. There are almost equal number of diamonds in every Cut category (except ‘Fair’) that are priced less than $2,000 and that are priced between $2,000 to $ 4,000.

The number of diamonds in the ‘Ideal’ category decreases drastically above $4,000. Further, in every category of diamond Cuts there are at least some diamonds that are highly priced.
