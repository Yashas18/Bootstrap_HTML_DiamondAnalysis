Diamond Count by Colors and Cuts

From the chart, we observe that in the distribution of 54,000 diamonds, most of the diamonds lie in the bucket of Color G (which is medium desirable on the scale of Color) and Cut ‘Ideal’ (which is the most desirable Cut).

We also notice a more prominent reduction in the number of diamonds as we move from ‘Ideal’ Cut to ‘Fair’ Cut than we do notice while we move across Colors from G on the either end of the spectrum. Hence, we can possibly conclude that ‘Cut’ is a more important feature as compared to the Color while analysing and valuing diamonds.
