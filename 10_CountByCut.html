Diamond Count by Cuts

The bar chart shows the count of the diamonds across various cuts in the set of 54,000. We notice that the ‘Ideal’ Cut (which is the most desirable Cut) occupies the highest spot in terms of absolute count (more than 20,000), whereas the ‘Fair’ Cut (which is the most desirable Cut) has the least number of diamonds in its bucket.
