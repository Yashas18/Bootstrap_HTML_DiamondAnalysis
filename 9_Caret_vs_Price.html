Diamond Carat vs Price

The Carat vs Price distribution depicts a positive relationship between the two parameters. A higher Carat would typically command a higher price.

Also, we notice that most of diamonds are clustered towards lower Carat and Price range. Finally, the diamond with highest Carat (of approx. 5) does not have the highest price.
