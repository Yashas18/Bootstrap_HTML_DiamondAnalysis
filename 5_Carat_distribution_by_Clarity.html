Diamond Carat Distribution by Clarity

The width of the box plot corresponds to the number of diamonds in the respective category. I1 is the least desirable whereas IF is the most desirable in terms of Clarity.

From the chart, we can observe that as we move from I1 to IF, the median Carat count decreases, which possibly implies that Clarity shares an inverse relationship with the Carat count.

Also, we note that I1, the least desirable Clarity, has the widest range of Carat distribution, with the highest number of outliers and fewest diamonds amongst all the buckets. The diamond with the highest Carat count (of approx. 5) lies in this category.

The maximum numbers of diamonds are shared within the buckets of SI2, SI1, VS2 and VS1. IF, which is the most desirable category, has the 2nd least number of diamonds along with a narrow range of distribution implying higher symmetry in the diamonds belonging to this group.
