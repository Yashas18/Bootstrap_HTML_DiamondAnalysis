Diamond Price distribution by Clarity

The Bin Size of the histogram is $2,000. Based on a split defined by Clarity, there are more diamonds in the range of $2,000 to $4,000 than in the range of less than $2,000 for all the categories except for VVS1 and IF, which are most desirable Clarity buckets. The number of diamonds in VVS1 and IF consistently decreases with increase in the price.

The number of diamonds in the Clarity category SI2 increases sharply with the price until $6,000 before it starts declining. Finally, in every category of diamond Colors there are at least some diamonds that are highly priced.
